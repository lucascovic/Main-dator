About us

"Who said marketing isn't art?"

European Trading Agency strive to becoming the largest trading platform for the retail industry with over 12 years experience in design, marketing & retail. The main focus is to offer suppliers, startups, retailers and selected entrepreneurs help and guidance in connecting with each other. Finding the right tools for expansion and export in different markets around Europe.

We can also help you with branding and marketing that fits the European market and your target customers. Together, we thoroughly go through your progress so far and give you professional advice on marketing strategies and improvements, based on your results. See this as mandatory preparations for the upcoming stages of your development and expansion in your market areas.

Together with you - we create a deeper development plan for the upcoming months & years to come, by connecting your business with the right network(b2b) and setting the future structure through the right suppliers/products/services you need. Coaching the business team individually to reach next milestone.

One of the biggest success indicators are expanding through collaborations with influencers, models, role models or companies with the same target interest and customer nisch as yourself. Our platform aims to give you the right tools for creating a collaboration that leads your brand to success.

Events

"Who said marketing isn't art?"

European Trading Agency loves to plan, develop and create events that will boost your brands and business! Over the years, we have created eye-catching & business-boosting events (b2b & b2c) all over Europe, starting from Spain, Paris & Latvia to end up in our popular capital city (where everything started) in Stockholm, Sweden.

Creating an event in the 20’s is not hard at all. With all the kinds of tool and technology we have today, it’s easy to build your dreams physically. But the key is not to build an event and stop there, the key is to build an event that captures your message without you saying it out loud. And that is our expertise. No matter how colorful it is.

Paris Texworld 2017

After attending & participating in the Paris Texworld 2017 show, founder of European Trading Agency – Diana Svensson, was announced as the Star of Texworld after building up a record long list of network and suppliers to work with. This was just right after she was praised for her record of having over 40 meetings in just 48 hours!

Paris Texworld later wrote: “Diana Svensson is an example of entrepreneurship and get-it- done attitude. Along with other BBB participants, her experience at the BBB-Texworld event shows how matchmaking and internationalisation can be an added value to European SMEs.”

Clas Ohlson 100-year anniversary

As a Nordic Marketing Executive, Diana has been working with many different world leading brands and customers around the globe. One of them has been the Swedish retail chain Clas Ohlson, where the brand Snazaroo is currently being sold. Snazaroo wanted to up their sales & participate in a colorful way – in a way that no one could miss! This resulted in a color explosion on 3x3 meters in front of the flagship store in Insjön, Sweden. Professional Face painters were hired in, the tent got covered in branded balloons and a campaign competition on Instagram started for all the parents that visited the event. This led to more online and in-store sales, greater visibility on social media and a great way to communicate about the news the brand just launched.

Designer Popup at The Lobby Stockholm

December 2019, the iconic brands of European Trading Agency; Peek-A-Poo & WHTE STHLM, premiered with their first popup store ever at The Lobby Stockholm on the popular street at Regeringsgatan 61. The designer popup was going strong until middle of January where WHTE STHLM premiered their very first Fashion show, to show the audience the edgy (and limited) ICEBAR Collection that launched just a week after the show.
